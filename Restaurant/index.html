about us

what makes our tea special?

At Tea-riffic, our green tea is meticulously sourced and brewed to perfection, ensuring a delicate flavor and vibrant aroma that captures the essence of each leaf. Our matcha undergoes a traditional Japanese stone-grinding process, resulting in a velvety smooth texture and rich, earthy taste that's truly incomparable.

From the fragrant spices of our chai to the bold complexity of our oolong, each cup is crafted with care and expertise, reflecting our dedication to providing a truly exceptional tea experience.

At Tea-riffic Tavern, we are honored to have received numerous accolades for our commitment to excellence in tea brewing.
